About Japanese

Japanese is a language spoken in Japan, a country in East Asia.

It is spoken by approximately 123 million speakers as of 2020.

Japanese is an agglutinative language. Its grammar is drastically different from English.

The writing system for Japanese are hiragana, katakana, and kanji.

While it may seem intimidating, anyone can master it through consistent practice.
